Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between ASP.NET Zero and ABP.IO?

ASP.NET Zero offers a modular application template for rapid development, while ABP.IO is a modern, flexible framework that supports microservices and modular architecture.

Do I need prior experience with ASP.NET to take this training?

Basic knowledge of ASP.NET is recommended, but foundational concepts will be covered to help beginners get started.

Are there discounts for group training?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 5 or more participants. Contact us for custom pricing.

What add-on modules are available?

You can choose from add-on modules like AI Chatbot with Azure OpenAI, Single Sign-On (SSO) with Azure AD, and Dynamic Theming for UI customization.

What is included in the ASP.NET Zero training?

The training covers multi-tenancy, modular architecture, CRUD development, dependency injection, and real-time notifications. A hands-on project is also included to reinforce learning.

Can I take individual ABP.IO modules?

Yes, ABP.IO training is modular, and you can enroll in specific modules based on your requirements.

What is the duration of the training programs?

ASP.NET Zero training is 24 hours, while ABP.IO training is modular, with each module lasting 8 hours.

Will I receive a certificate after completing the training?

Yes, participants will receive a certificate of completion after successfully completing the training program.

Is this training conducted online or in-person?

The training is conducted online to accommodate participants from various locations. Live sessions ensure real-time interaction with trainers.

What support will I get after the training?

Post-training support includes access to resources, recorded sessions, and a dedicated Q&A channel for addressing any additional queries.
